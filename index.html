Research

Publications.

Published May 2026 Wharton Sports Analytics Journal

Beyond the Expert: An Algorithmic Approach to Correcting Expert Bias in Fantasy Football Projections

Original research evaluating whether machine learning can systematically outperform expert cognitive bias in fantasy football projections. Position-specific XGBoost models were trained on ten years of NFL data with over 1,900 engineered features. By targeting and correcting known biases, the models improved ordinal rankings — particularly for quarterbacks and tight ends — versus ESPN's expert projections.
